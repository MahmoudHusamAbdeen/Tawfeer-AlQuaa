# Tawfeer Documentation

## Project Name

**Tawfeer — Smart Food Waste Reduction and Donation Platform for Al Qua'a**

## Overview

Tawfeer is a mobile application designed to reduce food waste, support people in need, and encourage sustainable food reuse in Al Qua'a. The platform connects homeowners, restaurants, supermarkets, farms, institutions, drivers, administrators, and government entities in one organized digital system.

The idea of Tawfeer is based on a simple problem: some food is still safe and useful, but it is wasted because there is no clear system to donate, collect, verify, deliver, reuse, or track it. Tawfeer solves this by providing a complete food waste management workflow.

The platform allows users to donate food, request food support, or reuse leftover and uncooked food using artificial intelligence. It also includes administrator approval, driver assignment, quality control, reward points, and a government dashboard for sustainability tracking.

## Business Plan Summary

Tawfeer is designed as a smart food waste reduction and donation platform. It focuses on three main problems:

1. Large amounts of food waste from homes, restaurants, supermarkets, farms, institutions, and gatherings.
2. Food insecurity among people or families who may need food support.
3. Lack of one organized digital system for donation, food request, AI reuse, delivery, quality control, and sustainability monitoring.

Tawfeer provides a complete solution by linking donors, recipients, drivers, administrators, and government monitoring in one ecosystem.

## Core Services

### 1. Food Donation System

The food donation system allows users to donate cooked or uncooked food. The donor provides food details, safety information, a photo, contact phone number, and current location.

After submitting the donation, the administrator reviews the request. If the food is accepted, it goes through quality control before delivery. The donor receives reward points after the donation is completed.

These points can be used later for discount cards or partner store benefits, which encourages people to donate instead of throwing away extra food.

### 2. Food Request System

The food request system allows people in need to request food support. The user provides the reason for the request, such as job loss, financial difficulty, or personal circumstances.

The user can also mention allergies or food restrictions, such as fish, lactose, or other ingredients. The request is reviewed by the administrator before assigning suitable food and delivery.

This keeps the process organized, respectful, and safer for the person receiving food.

### 3. AI-Powered Recipe Generator

The AI recipe feature helps users reuse food before it expires. The user enters the ingredients they already have and whether the food is cooked or uncooked.

The AI then provides a step-by-step recipe using the available ingredients. This helps families reduce waste at home by turning leftover or near-expiry food into useful meals.

## Food Safety and Quality Control

Food safety is one of the most important parts of Tawfeer.

When food is donated, it is reviewed based on its condition:

### Safe Food

If the food is safe for human consumption, it moves to quality control. The food is checked to make sure it is clean, safe, and suitable before it is packaged and delivered to people in need.

### Not Safe for Humans but Suitable for Animals

If the food is not suitable for humans but can still be safely used by animals, Tawfeer redirects it to animal feed.

### Not Consumable by Humans or Animals

If the food cannot be consumed by humans or animals, it is redirected to sustainable uses such as:

* Clean energy production
* Organic fertilizer
* Composting

This means Tawfeer reduces waste even when food cannot be eaten.

## Operations Plan

Tawfeer operations are divided into two parts:

### Front-Stage Operations

These are the operations visible to users:

* Donors submit food donations with photos, descriptions, safety information, and location.
* Recipients submit food requests with reasons and allergies.
* Drivers receive pickup and delivery tasks.
* Users receive status updates.
* Donors receive reward points.
* Government users view dashboard statistics.

### Back-Stage Operations

These are the internal operations:

* Admin reviews donations and requests.
* Admin assigns drivers.
* Quality control checks donated food.
* Unsafe food is redirected to animal feed, clean energy, organic fertilizer, or compost.
* The system updates statistics.
* Reports are prepared for monitoring and sustainability tracking.
